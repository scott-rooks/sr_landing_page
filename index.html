A little bit about me.

I've lived in Roswell, Georgia with my wife and four children for almost 25 years. My journey into tech started in the MSCE program. Then in 2008 I started a small food business with my wife Joanne. I began to blog and write online; I was fascinated with the internet; I was hooked and wanted to learn how to build my own site to fit my own specific dreams.

I learned how to code and actually built my first website. Then learned all about html5 and css3, which opened a new and passionate world in my work. Now I've made a full commitment to front-end development, where I can use my organizational and small business skills, while writing clean, elegant and responsive code for other small businesses.
